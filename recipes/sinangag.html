Pinoy Sinangag

Description

Sinangag is the perfect use for day-old rice.
Loaded with fragrant garlic bits, this Filipino-style fried rice makes a delicious and filling dish for breakfast.
Plus, this fried rice is super easy to make in minutes and with only 5 simple ingredients.
Just day-old steamed rice, oil, garlic, salt, and pepper, and youâ€™re on your way to a hearty and flavorful side dish!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cold and cooked white rice
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 head garlic

Steps

  1. In a wok or pan, heat up your cooking oil.
  2. Saute the garlic until it turns light brown.
  3. Add your rice and mix vigorously.
  4. Sprinkle generously some salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Best served hot. Share and enjoy!
